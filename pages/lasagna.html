Lasagna della mamma

The combination of tender pasta, savory meat sauce, and creamy béchamel creates a harmonious blend of flavors that will satisfy your taste buds. Each bite is a comforting and indulgent experience that will leave you craving for more. Whether you're a fan of traditional lasagna or prefer a vegetarian version, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household!

Below, you'll find the recipe for this delicious lasagna that will transport you to the heart of Italy with every bite!

Featured Ingredients :

12 Lasagna sheets
300g of Ground Beef
800g of Tomato Pulp
1 Onion
1 Clove of garlic
100g of Emmental Cheese
50g of Parmesan Cheese
4tbsp of Olive oil
Salt & Pepper
Herbs of Provence

How to Make Lasagna :

→ Follow these simple steps to create a delicious lasagna:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Cook the lasagna sheets according to the package instructions.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large pan and add the ground beef.
  4. Cook until browned, then add the onion and garlic.
  5. Add the tomato pulp and herbs, and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Layer the pasta sheets with the meat sauce and cheese.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
