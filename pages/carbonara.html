Spaghetti alla Carbonara

The combination of crispy pancetta, creamy egg sauce, and perfectly cooked pasta creates a harmonious blend of flavors that will satisfy your taste buds. Each bite is a comforting and indulgent experience that will leave you craving for more. Whether you're a fan of traditional carbonara or prefer a vegetarian version, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household!

Below, you'll find the recipe for this delicious carbonara that will transport you to the heart of Italy with every bite!

Featured Ingredients :

150g Pancetta
3 Large Eggs
50g Parmesan Cheese
Freshly Ground Black Pepper
400g Spaghetti

How to Make Carbonara :

→ Follow these simple steps to cook a delicious carbonara:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and a generous amount of freshly ground black pepper until well combined.
  4. Return the skillet with the pancetta to low heat. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to combine with the pancetta.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat and quickly pour the egg mixture over the spaghetti, tossing vigorously to coat the pasta evenly. The residual heat from the pasta will cook the eggs and create a creamy sauce.
