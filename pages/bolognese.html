Spaghetti alla Bolognese

The combination of rich meat sauce, aromatic herbs, and perfectly cooked pasta creates a harmonious blend of flavors that will satisfy your taste buds. Each bite is a comforting and indulgent experience that will leave you craving for more. Whether you're a fan of traditional bolognese or prefer a vegetarian version, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household!

Below, you'll find the recipe for this delicious bolognese that will transport you to the heart of Italy with every bite!

Featured Ingredients :

500g Ground Beef
400g Tomato Sauce
1 tsp Italian Herbs
1 Onion
2 Cloves of Garlic
400g Spaghetti
Olive Oil
Salt and pepper
Emmental

How to Make Bolognese :

→ Follow these simple steps to cook a delicious bolognese:

  1. In a large pan, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant.
  2. Add the ground beef to the pan and cook until it is browned and cooked through. Break up any clumps of meat with a spoon as it cooks.
  3. Once the beef is cooked, add the pancetta to the pan and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until it becomes crispy.
  4. Pour in the tomato sauce and sprinkle in the Italian herbs. Stir everything together and let it simmer for about 20-30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  5. While the sauce is simmering, cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.
  6. Once the sauce has thickened and developed rich flavors, serve it over the cooked spaghetti. Garnish with freshly grated emmental cheese if desired, and enjoy your delicious spaghetti alla bolognese!
